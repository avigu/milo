Three budget scenarios · pick one based on where you are

Phase 1 · MVP / Demo

Scrappy

~$0–$80 /month
Prove the concept, ~100 beta users, delayed data is fine.
  • Alpaca free (prices + 10yr history)
  • FMP free tier (fundamentals + analyst)
  • Marketaux free (news + basic sentiment)
  • Claude Haiku (~$30/mo for Aria text)
  • Supabase free · Vercel free
Limit: rate limits (~250 req/day each); US stocks only; 15-min delayed.
Phase 2 · Public launch · recommended

Real product

~$350–$700 /month
1k–10k users, real-time US data, full "why" engine.
  • Polygon Stocks Developer — $79
  • FMP Premium — $99
  • Marketaux Standard — $50
  • Claude Haiku at scale — ~$200
  • Supabase Pro + Vercel Pro — ~$70
  • Sentry + misc — ~$30
Sweet spot. Real-time quotes, all 5 strategies fully powered, AI reasoning on every pick.
Phase 3 · Scale / pro tier

Institutional

~$3k–$10k /month
100k+ users, push-notification news, power-user chat.
  • Polygon Advanced — $200
  • FMP Ultimate — $249
  • Benzinga News + Ratings — $1k–$3k
  • Claude Sonnet 4 power chat — $2k–$4k
  • AWS infra — $1k–$3k
Only if: you hit product-market fit and have revenue. Most of phase 3 cost is AI chat + premium news.
Caveats & notes: Prices are published list prices as of April 2026; vendors negotiate, and commercial redistribution of market data often needs a separate licensing agreement (FMP, Benzinga, Polygon exchange fees) — read the fine print before showing data to end users. For Israeli / European markets add EODHD ($60–$80/mo) — broader non-US coverage than Polygon. Alpaca doubles as execution: if you later want to trade inside the app instead of deep-linking, you can — commission-free, same API. Hot tip: Polygon's free plan is great for dev, but has 5 req/min — do not ship production on it.
