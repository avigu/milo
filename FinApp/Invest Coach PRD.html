What is Invest Coach?
Invest Coach is a stock-picking app that helps retail beginners find investment opportunities without needing to be financial experts. It runs 5 rule-based strategies against a curated universe of ~400 NASDAQ stocks every night, scores each one, and surfaces a ranked opportunity list with plain-English reasoning powered by an AI assistant called Aria. The app recommends — it does not execute trades.
One-sentence pitch
"Find the right stock to buy — and understand exactly why."

4 · The 5 strategies
Each strategy is a rule-based scoring pipeline. Strategies are not mutually exclusive — one stock can qualify under multiple strategies simultaneously.
1 · Bounce-Back Medium risk
A good company had one bad quarter and the stock overreacted. History suggests recovery in 3–6 months.
Signal conditions (all must pass)
  • Revenue YoY growth > +8% for ≥ 3 of last 4 quarters (long-term health)
  • Latest quarterly EPS missed estimate by > 10% (the "bad Q")
  • Stock price down > 15% from its 60-day high (overreaction)
  • News sentiment score > –0.3 (no scandal, no structural issue)
Data sources
FMP Q reportsAlpaca priceMarketaux news
2 · Market Dip Medium-high risk
External shock (war, rate hike, macro panic) pushes whole market down. Quality stocks drop unfairly — buy the dip.
Signal conditions (all must pass)
  • Market index (SPY or QQQ) down > 5% in last 10 trading days
  • News trigger is macro/geopolitical — not company-specific (earnings, fraud)
  • Stock P/E below its 5-year average (quality — not just cheap)
  • Stock dropped more than the index by > 5% (overreaction vs market)
Data sources
Alpaca index + stock barsMarketaux macro newsFMP fundamentals
3 · Undervalued Low-medium risk
Classic value investing: the stock is priced below what the company is actually worth by the numbers.
Signal conditions (all must pass)
  • P/E ratio < industry average P/E by ≥ 20%
  • Revenue growing YoY > 0% for last 2 consecutive years
  • Free cash flow positive for last 4 quarters
  • Debt/equity < 2.0 (not a value trap due to debt)
Data sources
FMP income, ratios, cash flowFMP analyst targets (fair value)
4 · Steady Dividend Low risk
Reliable income investors. Companies with 10+ years of raising dividends, low debt, recession-proof.
Signal conditions (all must pass)
  • Dividend raised every year for ≥ 10 consecutive years
  • Payout ratio < 65% (room to keep paying)
  • Debt/equity < 1.5
  • Current dividend yield ≥ 2.5% (above 10-year Treasury)
Data sources
FMP dividend history + balance sheetAlpaca price (for yield calc)
5 · Analyst Upgrade Medium risk
3+ professional sell-side analysts raised their price target in the last 30 days. The smart money is moving.
Signal conditions (all must pass)
  • ≥ 3 Buy / Outperform ratings issued in last 30 days
  • Average price target raised vs 30 days ago
  • Current price < average analyst target by ≥ 10% (margin of safety)
  • Downgrades in same period < 2 (no split opinion)
Data sources
FMP analyst upgrades + price targetsAlpaca price

6 · Aria — AI reasoning layer
What Aria is: A supporting AI assistant, not the decision-maker. The scoring engine makes the pick. Aria translates the score into human language and answers follow-up questions. Rule-based scoring is the source of truth.
Aria's jobs
JobInputOutputModel
One-liner "why" per pickScorer output + news headlines1–2 sentences shown on cardClaude Haiku (cached 24h)
Full reasoning breakdownAll 4 data source signals for the stockBullet points on stock detail screenClaude Haiku (cached 24h)
Chat / follow-up questionsConversation history + stock contextFree-form conversationClaude Haiku (live)
Market-wide summaryToday's full scan results"3 strategies are flashing — unusual" calloutClaude Haiku (nightly)
Aria guardrails
  • Aria never makes a price prediction ("this will go up") — only explains what the data shows
  • All Aria text is grounded in real scorer data — no hallucinated statistics
  • Aria includes a disclaimer on first chat session: "I'm not a financial advisor"
  • If user asks "should I put all my money in X?" — Aria redirects to risk profile and diversification
Prompt strategy
System: "You are Aria, an investing coach for retail beginners. You explain investment ideas in plain English. You always cite which data source supports each claim. You never predict prices or guarantee returns. Tone: warm, clear, confident."

User context injected per call: ticker, strategy matched, score breakdown, last 3 news headlines, analyst target, price change.

9 · Scoring engine
Each strategy produces a 0–100 score. A stock must meet all binary gate conditions before scoring. The final opportunity list ranks by upside % (analyst target – current price), not raw score.
Score weights by strategy
StrategyWeight componentsMin score to surface
Bounce-Back Revenue strength ×0.4 · Drop size ×0.4 · News clean ×0.2 ≥ 65
Market Dip Market drop size ×0.3 · Stock overreaction ×0.4 · Fundamentals quality ×0.3 ≥ 60
Undervalued P/E discount ×0.35 · Revenue trend ×0.25 · FCF strength ×0.25 · Debt safety ×0.15 ≥ 70
Steady Dividend Streak ×0.4 · Payout safety ×0.3 · Yield ×0.2 · Debt ×0.1 ≥ 75
Analyst Upgrade Upgrade count ×0.35 · Target raise % ×0.35 · Price gap ×0.2 · Consensus ratio ×0.1 ≥ 68
Upside % calculation
For strategies with analyst targets (Bounce-Back, Market Dip, Undervalued, Analyst Upgrade):
upside % = (avg_analyst_target – current_price) / current_price × 100

For Steady Dividend:
upside % = annual_dividend_per_share / current_price × 100 (= yield)

Displayed as the hero number on Home screen cards. Maximum cap: 80% (filters out data errors).
Fit score
The "fit" score (0–100) shown on stock detail is the strategy score adjusted for the user's risk profile. Conservative users see dividend-weighted fit; aggressive users see momentum-weighted fit. Purely informational — does not change the ranking order.

12 · Risks & mitigations
RiskLikelihoodImpactMitigation
Pick accuracy is poor — users lose money Medium High Strong legal disclaimer. Track accuracy internally. Tune scoring thresholds using Phase 0 backtesting. Do not make price predictions.
FMP free tier hit (250 req/day) Low in Phase 1 Medium Smart caching (90-day fundamentals). Upgrade to FMP Premium ($99/mo) if needed — budgeted for Phase 2.
Aria hallucinates a statistic Low with grounding High All Aria prompts include structured scorer data. Aria is instructed to cite only injected data. No web search enabled.
Regulatory / legal liability (financial advice) Medium Very high Clear "not financial advice" disclaimer. Recommend only — no trade execution. Consult legal before Phase 2 launch.
News API sentiment misclassifies macro vs company event Medium Medium Use Marketaux category tags (geopolitical, earnings, macro) + fallback: if ambiguous, do not trigger Market Dip strategy.
User churn — opens once, never returns Medium High Push alerts for dip events. Daily "pick of the day" habit. Watchlist alerts keep users returning.
Legal note: This product surfaces publicly available financial data and algorithmic analysis. It does not provide personalised financial advice and must not be positioned as such. A legal review is required before any public launch or marketing that implies investment recommendations.
